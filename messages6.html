Epicenter community groups on all social platforms are the meeting place for our community of Freeman to unite in a constructive way as we develop technical, business, community, governance, and other aspects of the ecosystem. For this purpose, our community moderators aim to assure the following Code of Conduct:

1. Treat everyone with dignity and respect

No name calling. No personal attacks. There are special groups like Iron Sharpens Iron https://t.me/+6uUWX4fOwugyMTdh where people with conflicting opinions can sort out differences. Alternatively, community members are advised to DM each other to get to the bottom of personal conflicts rather than polluting community channels with ego defending banter which distracts us from our community goals.

2. Do not spam

This is not a place for subtle promotion of other projects. Epicenter coins and tokens are partnered with dozens of other crypto projects and such projects are discussed in context of Epicenter ecosystem development. People who post links to risky websites, seed words, ad campaigns, wallet addresses, free Ethereum giveaways, ICOs, etc. are usually banned/reported with all posts being deleted.

3. Stay on topic

Chat price and trade here: https://t.me/EpicCashTrading. The main Epic Cash community group is not the place for debating HODL, swing trading, "why price up/down," "What happened to price", etc etc. There are groups such as https://t.me/EpicOfftop @EpicOfftop for inspiring and non-controversial/non-divisive topics and places like https://t.me/EpicOfftopic @EpicOfftopic where people can raise controversial concerns and debate toxically divisive issues. But community groups are for the purpose of building our community and will not be polluted by off topic word vomit.

4. No FUD!

Disgruntled EPIC holders, past and present no-coiners, or competitive coin holders aiming to improve their position like to come here and disrupt our productive work activities. They have one motive and that is to come into our community and poison the well in order to advance their personal agendas. In conclusion...

Freemen are a community of volunteers. Personal attacks against us will not be tolerated.

If you find yourself banned and you think it unfair, message an admin to see about how to right any wrongs.

The Epic Cash blockchain protocol is designed to be censorship resistant and our DAO governance community will work to see that this endures. Meanwhile, Moderators of Epicenter community groups will persist vigilantly to assure a safe working place that protects the dignity and respect of all Freeman.
